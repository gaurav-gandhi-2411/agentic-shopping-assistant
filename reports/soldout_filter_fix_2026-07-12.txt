FIX 1 — sold-out Shopify items filtered at ingest (launch-critical)
Run: 2026-07-12, commit (this commit)

ROOT CAUSE: scripts/download_shopify.py's normalize() read variants[0].price for
every product but never checked variant availability — a product with ALL variants
sold out was kept in the catalogue exactly like an in-stock one, since this catalogue
is a static snapshot with no other stock signal. Confirmed live: a real search
returned 3 links, all sold out.

FIX: normalize() now drops a product if ALL its variants have available=false
(Shopify's public /products.json exposes this per-variant). A product with at least
one available variant is kept — a single sold-out size is not itself disqualifying.
